About

Thank you all for visiting my website. I deeply appreciate it. Now, let's get into it. I'm an online personal trainer from Michigan. I found my passion for fitness in 2011 when I had a class for advanced physical education. It's crazy looking back to my old pictures when I was 145 pounds.

I've come a long way since then and learned so much in life and fitness. Nowadays I help people reach their physique and health goals online. I get the honor to train some amazing people and helping you guys reach your goals has given my life true meaning. Thank you so much, everybody.

Step By Step How To Transform Your Physique. I'll Use Me As An Example.

  • Step One

    Determine Your Goals

    My goals are a lot different than last year. I don't want to be a slave to the lifestyle right now. I'm eating anything I like (pizza, cereal, burgers, etc.) So long as my total calories keep me in a deficit and I have a fruit and veggie shake (for nutrients, vitamins, and minerals.) then I'll reach my goals. Which is simply to get leaner. This is NOT a good plan if your goal is bodybuilding for example.

  • Step Two

    Adjust Your Diet

    Once your goals are determined now we must adjust the diet. Are you looking to maximize your results or simply just get in better shape and be healthy? Your level of commitment with the diet will determine 70%+ your results. For example hitting your macronutrients and timing them perfectly will give you better results than just only counting calories.

  • Step Three

    Add Exercise

    This is my favorite part. We must pick a routine that you enjoy doing (cardio, weight training, HIIT, etc.) and adjust it to your fit your allotted days per week you have to train. It could be training 2 days a week to every day. If you're looking to build muscle fast then a higher volume higher frequency program might be better. If you're a track runner then we must having the proper amount of cardio and strength work while optimizing recovery. It's all based on your goals.

  • Step Four

    The Reward

    At this point you've set your goals, ate accordingly, and trained hard. By now you've established good habits and are changing your lifestyle! This is amazing and you should feel proud of all your hard work. When you look and feel better it seeps into other aspects of your life. Before you know it you're setting goals in life and attacking them methodically just as you did with your physique and health.
